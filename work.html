Project 1 - Drawing and Filmmaking

Filmmaking is another medium that, like photography, shares some commonalities with drawing. Drawing is in fact a vital part of filmmaking that happens behind the scenes. Storyboarding and production design are two areas in which artists utilize drawing to aid in storytelling.

Project 2 - Arbitrary Color

While local color refers to natural colors, arbitrary colors refer to unnatural or unordinary colors. The word arbitrary literally means "random." Artists use arbitrary colors in their work to upend traditional ideas about color. Historically the Fauves in France used arbitrary colors in landscapes and portraiture that add vibrance and contrast to traditional subjects.
