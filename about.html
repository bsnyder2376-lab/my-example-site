The path that led me here.

Were art becomes personality.

Brooke Snyder

Though I am originally from Tucson, AZ. My family came to Southern California when I was an infant. I stayed in the San Gabriel area for the better part of my youth and have remained in San Dimas for over 20 years. I started my College journey in 1995 at Citrus College, and now that I have enrolled again to complete my Associate Degree in Visual Arts, I realize that this is the best time for me to focus on my career now that my two children are grown. My daughter is 26 and has started a career in the Medical Field, and her Fiance is in the Pharmaceutical Field. My son, 21, is now living in Ogden, UT. He moved there in early 2025 and is working in the Aerospace Industry.
